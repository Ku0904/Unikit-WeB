Introduction

Unikit is like a one-stop solution for all your school or college needs. It has everything you might need for your studies, like notes, books, syllabus, past papers, and more.

Problem Statement

Students often struggle to find all the study materials they need in one place, resulting in wasted time and effort.

Solution

Unikit brings everything together in one place. You just need to select your college and course, and you'll find all the resources you need.
